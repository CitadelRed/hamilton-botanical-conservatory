What’s Happening at The Conservatory

We have lots of cool events for everybody! Compete in the events if you’re in the age group and bring someone to cheer you on! Non-competitive events like our Yoga classes are for everyone to enjoy!

See All Events

Pickle-Thon Pickle Rolling Tournament

The rules are simple – find a kid and bring him. He will be given a pickle and a toothpick. He must use the toothpick to roll the pickle without puncturing it and without touching the pickle with his hands. He must get the pickle across the finish line to win the pickle trophy. No betting is allowed on the results of the tournament.

  • Children 12 and under

  • At HBC

  • May 9th, 2024, at 6pm

Yoga by the Waterfall

Ground yourself among the plantlife as Yogi Kiersten Swanson takes you through the poses. Truly an amazing experience for any age. Yoga classes are 1 hour, bring your own yoga mats, yoga attire is suggested but not mandatory. Due to the yearlong temperate climate of the greenhouses, Yoga classes go all year long.

  • All Ages

  • At HBC

  • Weekdays at 8am, all year
