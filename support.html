We Have Plenty of Ways You Can Give

The Conservatory needs your support, and the more you can support us, the more of an impact we can make. You can donate money, volunteer for us, you could become a member of our not-so-secret plant society, there’s lots of ways you can make a difference by growing plants.

Donate

The Conservatory is dedicated to teaching people how to take care of our Earth with plants we can grow. Our business can also grow as well to serve and teach more people. You can donate once using our sweet online donation platform, or you can also donate to pay homage to a lost loved one if their will or estate mentions donation to us. If it does, their name can go up on The Homage Wall on our website. We thank you for your support and thank you for your financial friendship with us.

Learn More

Become a Member

The Conservatory’s members provide donations every month. They get free membership and free attendance at our events. You also get free parking in the parking lot, and member-only deals in the gift shop.

Membership Info

Volunteer

Volunteers help us keep our plants alive and spread plant knowledge. Our volunteers can be plant gurus, or, if not, they're there to water plants, run the events, run the gift shop, or manage the sanitation of the building. Regardless, a volunteer role is a very fulfilling role, it looks great on a resume.
