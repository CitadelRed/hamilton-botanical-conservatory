About Us

Our Mission

Hamilton Botanical Conservatory strives to preserve common and endangered plants while teaching our visitors and inspiring them to grow these plants to help our planet.

Our Vision

Hamilton Botanical Conservatory will become the best place to take your girlfriend and family, as we spread the knowledge and celebrate growing these beautiful organisms as a great way we can help the planet.

Ethical Policies

We make a commitment to:

Accessibility - We laid down ramps and give away free wheelchairs and strollers to rent for the day. And we welcome everyone.

Responsible Stewardship – We use your money the way you would use your money if you were us.

Climate and Ecology Advocacy – We use sustainable plants to slow climate change and grow sustainable food sourced from the earth.

Partnerships – We join up with community organizations to help the planet with plants.

Life-long learning - We like to teach people how to grow plants, so ask a volunteer your various plant questions, plant concerns, and plant conundrums.
